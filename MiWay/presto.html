PRESTO Advantages
  • Loyalty: Use your PRESTO Card for 12 rides in one week and ride the rest of the week free
  • Convenience: No need to find the exact cash or the right ticket, and even set up functions to automatically add value or renew passes to ensure you always have funds to travel.
  • Flexibility: PRESTO is valid across multiple transit agencies in the Greater Toronto and Hamilton Area and Ottawa and allows transit riders to easily transfer between agencies.
  • Peace of mind: If a registered PRESTO card is lost or stolen, it can be replaced and the value restored.
  • Cost savings: Transit fare paid on PRESTO is less expensive than the cash fare. Transfer from and to GO Transit for only 80¢
