A Message From Hazel
Benefits of the Transitway
  1. Increased Reliability: On the transitway, your trip is less likely to be affected by traffic congestion, so you'll get where you need to go on time.
  2. Reduced Travel Time: Your trip time on the transitway will be faster in comparison to current transit options and travel by car. You'll spend less time commuting and more time doing the things you want to do.
  3. Greater Convenience and Comfort: Improved security and protection from the elements mean a more comfortable ride. Improved service information will provide you with the peace of mind and the convenience you want.
  4. Greater Accessibility: More transit means more and better options for those without access to a car. People with a car may start to leave it at home.
